Google Sheets Setup

One-time setup to save all data permanently to your Google Sheet.

Step 1. Go to sheets.google.com → create a new sheet named "Outdoors Log".

Step 2. Create two tabs at the bottom: name them Hunt and Fish.

Step 3. In the Hunt tab, row 1, add these headers:
Date | TripID | SpotName | Lat | Lng | WindSpeed | WindDir | FeelsLike | CloudCover | Precip | SoilMoisture | Birds | Miles
Step 4. In the Fish tab, row 1, add these headers:
Date | TripID | SpotName | Lat | Lng | WindSpeed | WindDir | FeelsLike | CloudCover | Pressure | PressureTrend | Fish
Step 5. Copy the Sheet ID from the URL (the long string between /d/ and /edit).

Step 6. Go to script.google.com → New Project → paste the Apps Script below → Deploy as Web App (access: Anyone).

Step 7. Paste both IDs below and save.
